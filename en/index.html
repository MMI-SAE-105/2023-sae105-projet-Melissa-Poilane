Synopsis

The continuation of Robert Zemeckis' famous science fiction franchise, “ Back to the Future ”,It picks up where the first film left off. Marty McFly and Doc Emmett Brown are entrusted with a new mission: travel to 2015 to prevent a disaster that threatens the McFly family.

The journey they embark on takes them to a time where technologies futuristic govern everyday life. However, they must face unforeseen complications, such as the theft of the sports almanac by Biff Tannen.

To set things right, they will embark on new time-traveling adventures, juggling paradoxes and journeys through different eras.

This iconic sequel in the series skillfully blends humor, action, and reflections on the impacts of time travel, while also providing a fascinating glimpse of futuristic technology from the 80s.

Back to the Future 2 Continues to captivate audiences with its originality and sense of adventure, prompting reflection on the significance of our choices in shaping the future.
