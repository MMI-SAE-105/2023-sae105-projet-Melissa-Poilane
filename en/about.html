About Me

My name is Melissa Poilâne, I am 19 years old, and I am a student in Multimedia and Internet Professions (MMI). Passionate about design and fascinated by time travel, I am embarking on a website project centered around an iconic movie quote. My choice is the famous line " Where we're going, we don't need... roads " from Back to the Future 2. Through my website, I invite you to explore the universe of this quote and discover all my creations. Join me on a journey through time to explore the world of the Back to the Future trilogy.

Project Explanation

The project involves developing a website with a focus on its mobile version. The site revolves around a famous movie line, becoming its central point.
Accessible in both French and English to reach a wide audience, the homepage features key articles discussing the original movie, its director, an associated book, and even related music.

The objective is to design an environmentally friendly site, aiming for a score " A " on ecoindex.fr. Artificial intelligence is used to generate content in both languages.
To conclude, the aim of this project is to create an entertaining and informative website centered around a movie quote, adopting a mobile-first approach while adhering to best project management practices.
