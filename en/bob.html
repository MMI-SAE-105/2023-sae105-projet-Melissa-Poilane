Bob Gale

By Mélissa Poilâne

Published on 10/25/2023

Bob Gale, the genius writer behind Back to the Future, is a brilliant storyteller, blending ingenuity and timeless humor.

Bob Gale is a screenwriter, producer and director American born in 1951.
He is best known for his successful collaboration with Robert Zemeckis, with whom he co-wrote the screenplay for the trilogy " Back to the Future. "

Bob Gale is the creative genius behind the fascinating storyline of" Back to the Future. " As a co-screenwriter, he crafted the story of Marty McFly and Doc's time-traveling adventures.

His vision helped make this trilogy a cornerstone of science fiction cinema.
The adventures of Marty and Doc are the fruit of his imagination, and the story he co-created remains etched in the history of cinema.
