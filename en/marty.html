Michael J. Fox

By Mélissa Poilâne

Published on 10/25/2023

Michael J. Fox, An icon of both cinema and television, he embodies perseverance and charm. His iconic role in Back to the Future is unforgettable.

Michael J. Fox, real name Michael Andrew Fox, is a Canadian actor born in 1961.
He won the hearts of the audience with his outstanding performance as Marty McFly in the trilogy" Back To The Future. "

Michael J. Fox plays Marty McFly, the protagonist of the saga.
He is the young time traveler who finds himself entangled in countless adventures throughout history. Thanks to his natural charm, humor, and talent, Fox brought this iconic character to life

His impeccable performance made Marty an unforgettable character in the culture popular .
