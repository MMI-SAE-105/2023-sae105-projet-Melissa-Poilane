The unforgettable music from Back to the Future: Main Title, Extended Version

By Mélissa Poilâne

Published on 10/25/2023

" Main Title, Extended Version ", composed by Alan Silvestri, a iconic music that captures the grandeur of the saga.

Orchestral music

Original film scores have power: that of transcend the movie's images, making them an inseparable part of the filmic experience. In Back to the Future, the most memorable musical element is called" Main Tilte, Extented Version ", an original composition of the famous composer Alan Silvestri.

This iconic musical theme plays a rolecrucial in the atmosphere of the film and in the immersion of the public in the universe of Back to the Future.

" Main Title, Extended Version " is an extended version of the main theme also created by Alan Silvestri for the first film. It is instantly recognizable as the signature sound of the trilogy

From the very first notes, it evokes a mixture of excitement, nostalgia ,and curiosity, much like the essence of the film itself. The music accompanies every key moment in the film, be it action sequences or emotional ones.

For example when the DeLorean starts to fly, " Main Title Extented Version " reaches its peak, creating a dramatic tension that blends extremely well with the excitement of time travel
In the composition of the music, the majestic brass and melodious strings evoke a sense of grandeur, highlighting the importance of the moment.

A bold soundtrack

Alan Silvestri is an internationally renowned composer, with his score for Back to the Future 2 being one of his standout works and its close association with the director Robert Zemeckis, he managed to create a soundtrack that seamlessly integrated with the screen. Silvestri successfully balanced science fiction, comedy, and adventure while also adding emotion to the film with the soundtrack.

One of the most striking characteristics of" Main Title, Extended Version "East her judicious use of power build-up. The composition begins subtly and gradually builds an increasing tension which reaches its paroxysm at the right moment. This musical construction enhances the impact of the film's most memorable moments and intensifies the emotion felt by the audience

Music from Back to the Future 2, in particular the " Main Title, Extended Version " has become a classic in film music. It is often performed at symphonic concerts dedicated to film music and is celebrated by fans of the franchise. It reminds us of the importance of music in creating an immersive and memorable cinematic experience. Its lasting impact makes it an essential element of the franchise's legacy.
